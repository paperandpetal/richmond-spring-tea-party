What to Expect

A community-built afternoon

This is a potluck-style, volunteer-powered spring gathering. Think garden party meets community picnic. We're building this together, and it's going to be something really special. Currently it is free since there is no venue. Ideally I want to keep it this way minus any vendor goods or encouraged gratuity for performers.

🫖

Tea & Treats

Community-contributed teas, baked goods, and savory bites. Homemade, storebought, seasonal, and delicious.

🎶

Live Music

Original performances from Richmond's own talent. Intimate, acoustic, and perfectly springy.

💐

Florals & Beauty

Big hats, floral dresses, and garden party energy encouraged. Come dressed for the occasion.

🤝

Community

Meet your Richmond neighbors. This event belongs to everyone — it's built by the community, for the community.

What to Wear

There's no strict dress code — but we love a moment. Garden party glam is always welcome.

🌸

Florals

Print dresses, floral accessories — go for it. The more blooms the better.

👒

Big Hats

Wide brimmed, fascinator, sun hat — hats are highly encouraged.

🩷

Pastels

Blush, lavender, sage, cream — soft spring tones are perfect.

👗

Feminine & Fun

Midi dresses, cottagecore, garden party — express yourself and have fun with it.

Most importantly — wear whatever makes you feel beautiful 🌷
